This is Snake Babu a 3 year old male.

This boy was rescued with fractured leg and multiple wounds on his body. Found dragging himself about in a neighborhood, he was unable to stand. Maybe thats why we named him 'snake'. He has recovered fully and is among the fastest babies at the shelter. We love to call him 'Snakooo'. An thletic handsome fella, this boy will win hearts. This boy loves to make friends.

This is Tiff a 12 year old female.

This was our first 3 rescue before FKF even came into existence. She was rescued from a dumpsite. She is definately an alpha dog as no one dares cross this girl. She is the calmest dog we've ever encountered as nothing moves her nor freigtens her. She has the calmest spirit. She is exteremely particulat about cleanliness and is an exteremly disciplined girl. An absolutely stunning beauty.

This is Tooku a 3 year old male.

This boy was left pouring with blood was found clinging to life after a horrific knife attack. We picked up pieces of him from an open field. He is not one our most handsome dudes. This boy is natrural star. Extremely friendly, eager to make friends, eager to please with striking white coat and a handsome face. He is indeed a sight to behold.

This is Toothu a 3 year old female.

This girl was found emanciated in a cage way to small for her where she couldnt even stand. We were told she was used for breeding but was dumped after she fell ill. She is very friendly. She is docile and avoids all confrontation. She has a very loving nature.

This is Vallie a 1 year old female.

Kumaran, Valli and Vellan are siblings. They were found in an open field with multiple fractured bones. They were about 3 months old when rescued. With no mother in sight, they were brought to the vet. We had almost given up hope, but these kids pulled through. The siblings (Kumaran, Valli and Vellan) are alpha dogs in the making. Loyal, faithful and disciplined. Easy to maintain and make for excellent faithfull companions.

This is Vanitha a 1.5 year old female.

Anita & Vanita are sisters. They were found in a wet market area with they skin scalding & covered in blood. Vet's best guess is they were beaten with a sharp object. At the point of rescue they were 2 months old. Immigrant workers said their mother was beaten to death but refused to give more information. Vanita is always expressive and will answer your questions in detail. She is full of energy & bubbly. She is always out for an adventure.

This is Vimala a 2 year old female.

Kamala and Vimala are sisters. Their mother was beaten and stoned to death. These kids were 4 months old when rescued. They were also beaten. Being young as they were, it was a miracle both sisters survived the cruel ordeal they had been through. Just like her sister, she is quiet yet friendly and playful girl. Very calm to be around, she is docile and eager to please. Both sisters are very adorable.

This is Zaff a 11 year old female.

This was our first 3 rescue before FKF even came into existence. She was rescued from a workshop, badly injured. She is a natural born babysitter, ever so calm, ever so loving, this girl is just pure beautiful soul.
