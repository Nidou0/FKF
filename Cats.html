This is Chiku a 6 year old female.

Chiku was found abandoned in a drain with multiple puncture wound. She was clos to death when she was rescued. Its a miracle she survived. She is very playful. Though she keeps to herself, she is loving at heart. She is a super active girl.

This is Kitty a 6 year old female.

This cat was found tied up and thrown onto a highway. This was one dangerous rescue for us as we were getting ready to run across the highway. It was was also beyond terrifying to watch the cat lay there as we asses if she is alive after every car passes by. Surprisingly, she didn't have much physical injury on her. She is an alpha cat who is dominant by nature. This is one girl no one wants to mess with. Although she is super friendly and trusting of humans.

This is Kunchai a 5 years old female.

She is a very friendly girl who loves to run around in the sun. Ever the athlete, she can do acrobatics to amuse us. Very adorable.

This is Mimi a 4 years olf female.
