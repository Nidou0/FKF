This is Kumaran a 6 year old male.

This boy was found with a broken face and lower lips missing. He was at risk of bleeding to death. Multiple babies of ours had to donate blood to save his life. In the end he pulled through. He is now our resident doorman, ever ready to greet guest. He is loving and calm. Always on the job to greet guest, he is a natural ambassador at heart.

This is Marimuthu a 9 month old male.

Bibi, Indrani, Kanmani, Marimuthu, Rama, Sintamani are 6 siblings abandoned outside our shelter. They were tightly tied up in a plastice bag and were severely malnourished and deyhdrated as we do not know how long they had been out there. This is part of the reason that FKF choose to keep its location private for the most part. This boy is exteremely friendly. He is a alpha male in the making though he is also very disciplined.

This is Nanthini a 2 year old female.

She was rescued at 2 months old with maggot infested body and open wounds on her head. She had very low chances of survival. Yet her recovery was quite fast. She is shy girl. Still a little wary of strangers. However, she opens up to those close to her with her very playful character.

This is Nikki a 6 year old female.

Nikki was found caged in an abandoned house. She was thin beyond believe, we were scared to even carry her during rescue lest something snaps. She is now a gorgeous young lady. She is an alpha dog. Very protective of her family she is an excellent loyal companion. She also is a natural babysitter who is loving towards the younger ones. A genuinely angelic soul.

This is Parimalam a 4 year old female.

This girl was found beaten close to death. We were informed by the local feeder that her newborn puppies were purposefully poisoned and run over by car. She was both hurt physically and emotionally. She is friendly girl. She is also very quiet. She is a very disciplined girl and so easy to care for.

This is Puteri a 3 year old female.

This girl was found tied to an old fence and left to wither. She was so weak that she couldn't even lift her head to drink water during rescue. We do not know how long she had been left there but some people nearby said it was more than two weeks. She is an adventurist. Always exploring new teriitories, its feels like the world is her playground. Cheerful and vocal, she is an active happy girl.

This is Puteri ledang a 3 year old female.

She was beaten by a group of local kids. This had been going on repeatedly everyday for more than a month before we received the complaint. She had nowhere to escape as the kids would hunt her down for play. She was badly damaged at the time of rescue. Its amazing her her faith in humanity has remained intact after all that she went through. She is sunshine to behold. Always a smile on her face, this girl is testament to the amazing capacity for love that dogs have.
