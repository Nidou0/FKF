What is our goal? Read our Story

FUR KIDS FARM IS A MALAYSIAN REGISTERED NON-PROFIT ORGANIZATION DEDICATED TO RESCUING SEVERELY ABUSED OR INJURED ANIMALS.

Rescuing these angels comes at a cost, we face financial containts that come in the form of veterinary costs, land costs and nutrition.
Rescue Cost: Each rescue averages RM3,000, straining our limited resources.
Beyond Physical Healing: The fight continues with lingering trauma, a phantom limb gnawing at their spirits.
Your Lifeline: Your support becomes crucial, providing the necessary resources for both rescue costs and ongoing care.

Dream: Build a permanent sanctuary for rescued animals on our land. Purpose: Provide a forever home filled with love, security, and a promise for a brighter future. Support Impact: Your contributions are the foundation for this sanctuary, each donation a brick towards building a home where broken spirits can mend. Collaboration: With you by our side, rewrite stories of abuse and neglect into a soaring anthem of love, sanctuary, and a future where no vulnerable soul is left behind.

What we want? Our Vision

Rehabilitation Center

Help us mend shattered limbs and broken spirits, not just for FKF animals, but for all in need.

Nourishment

Fuel their journey to health with premium food and essential supplements.

Sustainability

Help us establish a pet-related business, a gentle whisper of self- sufficiency that allows us to care for animals without constant reliance on sponsors.

Vet bills

Ensure each rescued soul receives the medical care they desperately need.
